Mild Fish Curry

Description

This is a sweet, tender and mild fish curry which will warm you up after a long day in the fields. It is made with a variety of spices that create a rich and aromatic sauce, perfect for pairing with rice or bread.

The fish it cooked until it is soft enough to fall apart at the touch, whereas the rice is kept sticky and fluffy, contrasting the smooth, thick texture of the curry

The curry itself has rich flavours and contains different vegtables and spices which create the ideal blend of flavours. You will truly never forget the taste of this extraordinary dish.

Ingredients

  • White fish
  • Coconut milk
  • Onion
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Red peppers

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add turmeric and red peppers, cooking until soft.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the fish and cook until it flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Stir in chopped coriander and serve hot with rice or bread.
